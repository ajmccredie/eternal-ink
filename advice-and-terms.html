Our Official Tattoo Aftercare Information

  • Keep the tattoo covered with a bandage or wrap for 2-3 hours after tattooing.
  • Gently wash the tattoo with mild soap and water 2-3 times a day for the first week.
  • Apply a thin layer of tattoo ointment or cream to the tattoo 2-3 times a day for the first week.
  • Avoid soaking in water for at least two weeks.
  • Keep the tattoo out of direct sunlight or tanning beds for at least two weeks.
  • Avoid picking at scabs, as this may damage the tattoo and lead to infection.
  • If there are any signs of infection, such as swelling, redness, or pus, seek medical attention immediately.

Terms and Conditions of Booking

  1. Deposits are required for all tattoo appointments and are non-refundable.

  3. No shows will result in the loss of deposit and the need to provide another deposit to book future appointments.
  4. In the event of lateness, the appointment will be subject to rescheduling and the loss of deposit.
  5. Customers are responsible for notifying the tattoo artist of any changes to tattoo design or placement at least 48 hours prior to appointment.
  6. The tattoo artist reserves the right to refuse service that is deemed inappropriate or offensive.
  7. Customers must be 18 years or older to receive a tattoo, and must provide a valid ID at the time of appointment.
